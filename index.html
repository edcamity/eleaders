WELCOME TO THE E-SUMMIT 16

eDC is a startup set in Amity University, Noida, which focuses on entrepreneurship and growing startup culture. We are conducting an e-Summit in the first week of April and are looking for passionate E-Leaders cum campus ambassadors for the same.

Deliverables Desired from the ambassadors.


  • Offline publicity – putting up posters provided by us, conducting workshops in your college to tell people about the summit.

  • Online publicity – posting on your Facebook walls, sharing the events in different college groups and broadcasting on WhatsApp groups and personal chats.

  • Getting people to register for the event, the ones who are really willing to attend the event.

BENEFITS FOR THE E-LEADERS

THE E-LEADERS WILL BE GIVEN INCENTIVES ON THE BASIS OF THE WORK DONE BY THEM.

  • 25% cut on each registration.

  • The ambassadors may further get a fixed amount of money for each referral that gets selected in the campus ambassador program.

  • T-shirts from eDC.

  • Certificates.

  • Free entry to the summit.

  • Discounts on workshops if they are able to pull up a decent number of registrations.
